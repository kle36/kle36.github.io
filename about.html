Our Obsession

Hermies is a respectful evolution of Filipino culinary traditions. The brand treats Filipino cuisine as a flexible medium for innovation and beauty. At Hermies, legacy is not just preserved, it is propelled forward.

Tom Cunanan

Head Chef

For Chef Tom Cunanan, inheritance began at home. The recipes he learned from his mother, Hermenia, were not simply instructions, but living knowledge and experience. These dishes built the foundation of his culinary identity, grounding him in the flavors, techniques, and traditions of Filipino cuisine from an early age.

As his career developed, that foundation expanded. Beyond the home kitchen, Tom absorbed techniques, flavors, and perspectives from chefs and cuisines around the world, an evolving education that extended far beyond what was first passed down to him.

Now, Chef Cunanan continues to refine his Filipino-American cuisine at Hermies, where his constant evolution allows him to reinterpret traditional dishes in fresh ways while staying rooted in authenticity, making Hermies not just a restaurant but a dynamic extension of his culinary journey.
