The Concept Foundation
✗ "Filipino fusion restaurant"
→ No. "Filipino cuisine, propelled forward."
→ Evolution, not fusion. Fusion erases. Evolution celebrates.
"The crossing of cultures shouldn't erase - it should illuminate what was already there."
✗ "modernize everything" too clinical
→ Modernize nothing. Reveal everything.
Mama's Kitchen The Origin
★ Every dish should answer: "does this taste like love?"
✓ The vinegar - always vinegar. It's the soul of Filipino food.
✓ Coconut milk = the base. Try lighter versions → NO. Full fat. Always.
✓ Ube is not a trend. It's memory. It's heritage. Treat it that way.
→ Pandan must be fresh. Dried = unacceptable. Frozen = unacceptable.
★ Calamansi instead of lemon, everywhere we can. (local Houston supplier found!)
She never measured anything. She cooked by memory, by smell, by the sound of the pan. That's what I'm chasing - that feeling of certainty that comes from years of love, not technique.
